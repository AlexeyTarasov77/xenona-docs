# Deployed factories

This section provides a comprehensive list of all officially deployed Xenona Factory contracts across the supported blockchain networks.
Each Factory contract serves as the central hub for creating, deploying, and managing individual giveaway contracts in a fully automated and verifiable manner. 
The Factory ensures that prizes are securely locked, entry rules are enforced, and winners are selected using provably fair randomness through Chainlink VRF.

By exploring this list, users and developers can verify the authenticity of each deployed Factory, examine network-specific details, and gain insights into how giveaways are managed on-chain.
This transparency allows anyone to confirm that the contracts they interact with are genuine and trusted.
Whether you are a giveaway creator or a participant, referencing the deployed factories helps you ensure that all prize handling, participant tracking, and random winner selection are executed securely and without any manual interference.
